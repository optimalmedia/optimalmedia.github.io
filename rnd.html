Supported research and technologies



  • The Code Version International

    Providing the development of non-commercial free independent international open platform for distributed version control systems (compatible with such systems as Git, Mercurial).


  • The copyright protection (NDA) open-source technology

    This technology allows automatic embedding of hidden unique user IDs into media-objects received by users. Each allocation of media-content generates a key, which allows the content owner to identify unauthorized copies of their content and demand compensation (including legal fees).


  • The media-objects distribution technology

    The technology implements geo-dynamic distribution of media-objects, which increases the speed of acquisition (downloading) of media-objects and decreases congestion in information highways.


  • The Mediagraph

    Mediagraph is an open technology which links media instances (predominantly documentaries and feature videos) with the objects they contain (a picture if it is a painting, a text if it is a book). The graph is a network of links between automatically-standardized hierarchies of descriptions (tags). A platform that stimulates a collective development of the Mediagraph is under way. The graph helps authors and producers of various content (works of art, goods and services) and media instances to collaborate effectively. This technology is meant to facilitate co-operation between individuals and organizations engaging in creative productive activities.
