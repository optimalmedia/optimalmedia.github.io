Supported research and technologies



  • The copyright protection (NDA) open-source technology.

    This technology allows automatic embedding of hidden unique user IDs into media-objects received by users. Each allocation of media-content generates a key, which allows the content owner to identify unauthorized copies of their content and demand compensation (including legal fees).


  • The media-objects distribution technology.

    The technology implements geo-dynamic distribution of media-objects, which increases the speed of acquisition (downloading) of media-objects and decreases congestion in information highways.


  • The Mediagraph.

    Is an open technology that links media-instances (now and in the future these are dominantly documentaries and feature videos) with the objects they contain (a picture if it is a painting, a piece of text if it is a book). The graph is a network of links between automatically-standardized hierarchies of descriptions (tags). A platform that stimulates a collective development of the Mediagraph is under way. The graph helps authors and producers of different material items (works of art, goods and services) and media instances collaborate effectively. this technology is to help easily build complex structures of co-operation between people and organizations in creative-productive activity.
