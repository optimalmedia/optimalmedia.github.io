Optimal Media Association International





  • We track research and development in the field of information technology, conduct comparative analysis, and MONITOR developments in International Law pertaining to content distribution.


  • We promote development of technologies which reduce costs and remove intermediaries in the field of information exchange and copyright protection, to further harmonious development in art, culture, science and technology.


  • We support technologies that are developed exclusively under open licenses (e.g. GPL 3.0), for open systems are the base of life itself.


  • We support research and development of new paradigms in information transfer intended to benefit society while preserving cultural traditions of the nation members of the Association.
